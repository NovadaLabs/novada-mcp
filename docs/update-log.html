FIX Scraper 状态竞态修复 Scraper Status Race Fix e708010

novada_scraper_submit 返回 task_id 后立即调用 novada_scraper_status,服务器尚未传播,返回 not_found。原响应未区分「竞态」与「无效 ID」。 Calling novada_scraper_status right after novada_scraper_submit hit a propagation window returning not_found. The old response didn't distinguish a race from a genuinely invalid task_id.

修复: not_found 的 agent_instruction 现区分两种情况 — 刚提交则等 5-10s 重试一次;重试后仍 not_found 则判定无效/过期。 Fix: not_found response now distinguishes two cases — just submitted: wait 5-10s, retry once; still not_found after retry: treat as invalid/expired.

Agent-First: 错误响应必须告诉 agent「下一步做什么」,不能只报失败。 Error responses must tell the agent what to do next, not just report failure.
